Nominations are now open for the 2021 Collibra Excellence Awards

The Collibra Excellence Awards honor teams and individuals who are helping their organizations unlock the value of data by collaborating with Collibra. The theme of the awards is “Making data meaningful.” Apply or nominate someone for the following awards by sharing your story and letting us know how you’re making an impact with data across your organization.

Apply below and share your story today! Nominations close March 5, 2021. Winners will be announced at Data Citizens ‘21.

Data Citizen of the Year

The Data Citizen of the Year award celebrates the fearless leader who has the vision to drive your organization to achieve Data Intelligence. Whether working on data or digital transformation projects that drive change and real business value, we want to hear about the success they achieved and how they played a key role in reaching that success.

Data Program of the Year

The Data Program of the Year celebrates the best of the best when it comes to data transformation programs. This organization had the vision and ambition to drive real business value, all powered by the Collibra Platform.

Customer Impact Partner of the Year

A new award in 2021, the Customer Impact Partner of the Year recognized the Collibra partner who has provided great value to our mutual customers including but not limited to driving a successful implementation, adoption and expansion of Collibra.

Note: Collibra's annual 'Partner of the Year Award' will be awarded to the partner that has the highest achievement of success for meeting and surpassing their tier requirements. No nomination is required.

Collibra Certified Ranger of the Year

The Collibra Certified Ranger of the Year celebrates the Collibra Ranger who has led their team in designing and implementing Data Intelligence programs that drive real business value.

What comes with the Collibra Excellence Awards?

Collibra Excellence Award winners will receive:

  • An opportunity to present their story during Data Citizens ‘21
  • A profile and story featured on the Collibra blog and our social channels to be seen by thousands of data professionals throughout the world
  • A chance to be interviewed for additional news and press
  • Recognition during the awards ceremony and a special award to bring back to their organizations

To apply, please fill out the form* below for the respective award(s) for which you would like to apply.

How will submissions be evaluated?

Submissions will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  1. How well the submission addresses the prompt—Making Data Meaningful: The nominee has helped an organization to unlock the value of their data by partnering with Collibra. (50%)
  2. Being able to demonstrate meaningful, measurable results: The nominee is able to quantify the business impact of their data strategy and/or highlight the business benefits. (25%)
  3. Knowledgeable and/or innovative use of Collibra (25%)
